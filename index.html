Langathlon

Welcome!

Langathlon is an online "read-a-thon"-like reading group where you read texts and get scored.

How this works

  1. ✋ Sign up for a session — Every week, all the session participants read the same material (for example, a book chapter, a textbook lesson, or a YouTube video, etc.)
  1. 📚 Read and submit a summary — After you read the material you submit a summary in the language you are learning. The summary will be scored automatically.
  1. 🏅 Get scored and discuss — You get a score for how much and how well you read. You optionally discuss what you read with other participants.

More details

  • One session typically lasts for 6-12 weeks, depending on how many units/weeks there are. We read one material (a book chapter, a textbook lesson, a video etc) per unit/week.
  • At the end of each week, you submit a summary via Google form. Summaries are due every Sunday at 11:59pm AOE.
  • Your summary will be scored automatically based on a machine learning algorithm. Your summary gets a higher score if 1) it is accurate and relevant summary of the reading material and 2) it is close to what other participants write. Your summaries won't be published.
  • After you submit your summary, you get a score, which equals to #(number of words/characters in the material) times #(the score of your summary, normalized to 0 to 1)
  • You get a 🎖️ if you complete all the units in a session. You'll get 🥇, 🥈, or 🥉 based on the total scores you get in the session.
  • We'll post a leaderboard with user names on this website, along with your badges. You can opt out from the public leaderboard.
  • We'll use Discord as the communication platform. You can discuss what you read in channels, but this is optional. Participants can also optionally organize synchronous meetups to discuss the material.
